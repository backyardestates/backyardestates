# Presenter Slides — Data Reference

This file describes what data lives on each of the 10 presenter slides. Use it to brainstorm layout and design without touching any code.

---

## Slide 1 — Cover

**Purpose:** First impression. Sets the tone for the whole presentation.

**Data on screen:**
- Customer's full name (large)
- Property address
- Property photo (full or partial background)
- ADU type (Detached / Attached / Garage Conversion)
- Today's date
- Contractor license number + dealership license number
- Tagline: "We build for you."
- Backyard Estates logo

---

## Slide 2 — Your Property

**Purpose:** Show the customer their own property and what we're proposing for it.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "Your Property" + property address
- Property photo (left side)
- ADU type label (e.g. "Detached ADU")
- List of floor plan options being compared — each shows: square footage + name (e.g. "1,200 sqft · The Cypress")
- Info blurb based on ADU type (e.g. "We handle the tie-in, firewall, and exterior matching.")
- Site findings tags — items flagged during site assessment (e.g. "Concrete Cut", "Tree Removal")

---

## Slide 3 — Your Options (Pricing Table)

**Purpose:** Show the price breakdown for each floor plan being compared side by side.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "Your Options" + ADU type · City
- Left sidebar: full list of what's included, grouped into 5 categories:
  - Services (plans, engineering, permits, project manager, portal, solar)
  - Construction (foundation, framing, insulation, water heater)
  - Interior (flooring, ceilings, HVAC, ceiling fans)
  - Kitchen + Bathrooms (cabinets, quartz counters, appliances, vanities)
  - Exterior (stucco, roof, windows)
- Right: one column per floor plan being compared (up to 3), showing:
  - Base unit price (animated count-up)
  - Pre-permitting & construction: "Included"
  - Additional site work cost (if any) + site work tags
  - Subtotal
  - Named discount lines (e.g. "Military Discount −$5,000")
  - **Total** (final price per unit)
- Footer note: offer valid 15 days, water meter allowance, tagline

---

## Slide 4 — See It In Person (Gallery)

**Purpose:** Show real completed ADUs Backyard Estates has built.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "See It In Person" + page counter (e.g. "1 / 3 pages")
- Grid of photos from completed properties (3 columns × 2 rows = 6 photos per page)
- Each photo is labeled with the property name on hover
- Auto-advances every 6 seconds (can be paused)
- Section label: "Real Homes We've Built"
- Pagination dots

---

## Slide 5 — What's Included

**Purpose:** Reinforce that the price is truly all-in. No hidden costs.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "One Price. Fully Turnkey." + "Attached, Detached & Garage Conversions"
- 4 cards in a 2×2 grid, each listing included items:
  - Card 1: Services + Construction
  - Card 2: Interior + Exterior
  - Card 3: Kitchen
  - Card 4: Bathrooms
- Bottom strip: "Potential Added Costs" — 17 tags of things that may cost extra (e.g. "Tree Removal", "Retaining Walls", "Impact Fees")
- Note: "Fully assessed before signing"

---

## Slide 6 — Your Investment (ROI Cards)

**Purpose:** Show the financial return on each floor plan — make the numbers exciting.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "Return on Investment" + "6.5% · 30yr · 100% financed"
- One card per floor plan (up to 3), each card shows:
  - Floor plan name + square footage
  - Financed: 100%
  - Out of Pocket: $0
  - Equity Boost Year 1 (animated)
  - Equity Boost Year 5 (animated)
  - Equity Boost Year 10 (animated)
  - Estimated Monthly Rent (animated)
  - Monthly Payment (animated)
  - **Monthly Cashflow** — large, colored green (positive) or red (negative), animated
  - **Annual ROI %** — largest number on the card, animated
- Disclaimer: "Backyard Estates does not make any guarantees regarding rental income or long-term value."

---

## Slide 7 — What Others Say (Testimonials)

**Purpose:** Social proof. A real customer story with a video option.

**Data on screen:**
- Photo of the completed ADU (left side, full height)
- Customer portrait (circle, with initials fallback)
- Customer name
- Purpose/motivation (e.g. "Rental Income", "Family Housing")
- Pull quote from the customer (up to 4–5 lines)
- Video thumbnail + "Watch their story" link (Wistia video)
- If no story is selected, shows a placeholder

---

## Slide 8 — The Return (ADU vs. Buying a House)

**Purpose:** Compare buying a house vs. building an ADU — show the ADU wins financially.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "ADU vs Purchasing a House"
- Comparison table with 3 columns: Line Item | House | Your ADU
- Rows (all animated):
  - Purchase Price
  - Down Payment (House: 20%, ADU: $0)
  - Remodel ($0 for both)
  - **Cost Out of Pocket** (House: large negative, ADU: $0)
  - Monthly Payment
  - Property Tax
  - Insurance
  - Maintenance
  - **Monthly Cost** (summary row)
  - Rent collected
  - **Cashflow** (House: negative arrow, ADU: positive arrow) — highlighted row
  - AVG Equity Boost (ADU only)
- Disclaimer

---

## Slide 9 — How It Works (Process + Payment Schedule)

**Purpose:** Show what happens after they say yes, and when money is due.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: "What Happens After You Say Yes"
- 5 process steps (icons + title + description):
  1. Sign & Deposit — DocuSign sent by tomorrow
  2. Site Visit — On-site within one week
  3. Floor Plan Review — Custom design, your input
  4. Permits & Plans — We handle everything
  5. Build & Keys — Weekly updates in BuilderTrend
- Callout: "You talk to us. We talk to everyone else."
- Payment schedule section — tab selector if multiple units:
  - Up to 10 milestone cards, each showing: milestone number, label (e.g. "Framing"), amount (animated)
  - Split into 2 rows (6 + 4)
  - Total card showing final price
- Footer: "Nothing is due until the milestone is completed."

---

## Slide 10 — What's Next (CTA)

**Purpose:** Close the presentation. Clear next steps and contact info.

**Data on screen:**
- Header: Backyard Estates logo + "Ready to move forward?" + phone number
- 4 next steps in a 2×2 grid:
  1. Sign & Deposit — "Start with a signed agreement and initial deposit"
  2. Schedule Site Visit — "We come to you within the week"
  3. Design Your ADU — "Customize your floor plan with our team"
  4. Break Ground — "Permits pulled, build begins"
- Timeline section — 3 phases with city-specific durations:
  - Pre-Construction (Backyard Estates: 2–4 weeks)
  - Permitting (city average, e.g. "Corona: 3–5 months")
  - Construction (Backyard Estates: 4–6 months)
- Footer:
  - Left: email + website
  - Center: QR code linking to website
  - Right: "We build for you." + contractor/dealership license numbers

## Notes for Design Brainstorming

- Slides 1, 2, 4, 6, 7, 9, 10 have **dark backgrounds** in the current direction
- Slides 3, 5, 8 are candidates for **light backgrounds** (data-heavy, easier to read on white)
- Every slide ends with the small tagline **"We build for you."**
- Brand colors: Teal `#2D5F5F`, Gold `#B8954A`
- Font: Outfit (Google Fonts) — loaded via CSS variable `--p-font`
- Dollar amounts on slides 3, 6, 8, 9 **animate from $0** when the slide becomes active
- The presentation is shown **fullscreen on a second display** during a sales meeting
- Navigation: arrow keys or clicks advance slides
